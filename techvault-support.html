Frequently Asked Questions

Our support team operates 24/7/365. SLA response times vary by priority level: Critical issues receive a 15-minute initial response, High priority receives 1-hour response, Medium priority receives 4-hour response, and Low priority receives next-business-day response. Enterprise clients may have custom SLAs in their service agreements.
To escalate an existing ticket, reply to your ticket confirmation email with "ESCALATE" in the subject line, or call our emergency line at 1-800-555-0199 and reference your ticket number. Our escalation team will review your case within 30 minutes and assign a senior engineer if warranted.
For fastest resolution, include: the exact error message or behavior observed, steps to reproduce the issue, when the issue first occurred, any recent changes to your environment, affected systems or services, business impact level, and any troubleshooting steps already attempted. Screenshots or logs can be attached via email response.
Yes, scheduled maintenance can be requested through the support portal or by submitting a ticket with "Scheduled Maintenance Request" in the subject. Please provide preferred date/time windows (include timezone), estimated duration, systems affected, and contact information for the maintenance window. We recommend 72-hour advance notice for non-emergency maintenance.
Monitoring dashboards are accessible through our client portal at portal.techvault.io using your organization's SSO credentials. If you don't have portal access, submit a ticket requesting access and include your company domain. Dashboard access can be configured for read-only or administrative privileges based on your role.
Security incidents are immediately escalated to our Security Operations Center (SOC). We follow a structured response: Detection & Analysis (triage within 15 minutes), Containment (immediate threat mitigation), Eradication (root cause removal), Recovery (system restoration), and Post-Incident Review (full report within 48 hours). Security tickets are automatically flagged as Critical priority.
